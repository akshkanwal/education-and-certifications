# Google Project Management Certificate

**Issuer:** Google (via Coursera)  
**Completed:** November 2023  
**Status:** Active

## What I Learned

- Project management fundamentals: project lifecycle, stakeholder management, scope definition
- Agile and Scrum: sprint planning, retrospectives, backlog management, Agile mindset
- Risk management: risk identification, assessment matrices, mitigation planning
- Communication: status reporting, stakeholder updates, escalation frameworks
- Project planning tools: Gantt charts, work breakdown structures, task dependencies
- Change management: managing scope creep, handling changes through formal processes
- Post-project review: lessons learned documentation, KPI measurement, close-out procedures

## How I Use This at Work

This certificate directly supported my transition into the **IT Project Coordinator role at F12.net**, where I managed end-to-end infrastructure projects. The project lifecycle framework, stakeholder communication templates, and risk management approach from this program became part of how I structured project delivery.

At **ESW IT**, the project management skills show up in how I scope consulting engagements, communicate timelines to clients, and coordinate with vendors and internal teams. Even on the technical side, thinking in projects — defining the outcome, identifying risks, communicating status — makes complex deployments more predictable and professional.
